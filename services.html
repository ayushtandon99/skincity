Abhyanga: Ayurvedic Oil Massage for Rejuvenation and Detoxification

Abhyanga is a deeply relaxing Ayurvedic oil massage designed to rejuvenate the body and balance your doshas (body energies). This ancient therapy involves applying warm, herbal-infused oils tailored to your body type, promoting detoxification, stress relief, and improved circulation.

Regular Abhyanga not only nourishes the skin but also enhances muscle tone, supports joint health, and boosts immunity. Experience the benefits of Abhyanga for holistic wellness, glowing skin, and total body rejuvenation.

Shirodhara: Ayurvedic Therapy for Stress Relief and Mental Clarity

Shirodhara is a deeply relaxing Ayurvedic treatment where a continuous flow of warm, herbal-infused oil is gently poured over the forehead, targeting the “third eye” area. Known for its powerful ability to reduce stress, anxiety, and insomnia, Shirodhara also promotes mental clarity and emotional balance.

This ancient therapy calms the nervous system, helps alleviate headaches, and improves sleep quality, making it ideal for those seeking mental rejuvenation and holistic healing. Experience the therapeutic benefits of Shirodhara for stress relief, relaxation, and enhanced mental focus.
